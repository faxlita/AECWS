Theater Play Script

From Struggle to Purpose

A story of faith, English, and perseverance.

Characters / Cast
  • Narrator: Fandry
  • Student 1: Eddie (shy)
  • Student 2: Holy (discouraged)
  • Bully 1: Sitraka
  • Bully 2: Lahatra
  • AEC Member: Oméga
  • Teacher: Isaia
  • Extras: Club Members, HR
Final Tips
  • Let students pause for laughter (very important!)
  • Use body language for humor (faces, reactions)
  • Add background music between acts
  • Keep energy high during club scenes

Act 1: Struggle

(≈5 min)

(Classroom. Students chatting loudly.)
Narrator Fandry

In a public high school, two students are trying their best... but life is not easy.

Sitraka (looking at Eddie): Hey... is that your uniform or your pajama collection?

Lahatra: Maybe it's "vintage"... from 2010!

(Students laugh.)

Holy (whispering): If this is fashion... we are very fashionable.

Eddie (small smile): Yes... very consistent fashion.

(Teacher Isaia enters)

Teacher Isaia: Today, we practice English! Who is ready?

(Everyone suddenly looks at their desk)

Teacher Isaia: Ah, I see... all of you became very shy today. Eddie, try please.

Eddie (nervous): My name is Eddie ... I like... study...

Sitraka (mocking): "My name is Eddiiiiiiiie..."

Teacher Isaia (firm): Sitraka, you laugh... but you didn't volunteer.

(Class: "Oooh!")

Holy (quietly to Eddie): At least you tried.

Eddie: Yes... but my English is running away from me.

Act 2: Opportunity

(≈4-5 min)

(Outside school. Holy and Eddie talking.)

Holy: Maybe we should stop trying English...

Eddie: Yes... English and I are not friends.

(Oméga enters, cheerful)

Oméga: Hi! I heard you in class.

Eddie (worried): You... also want to laugh?

Oméga: No! I want to invite you.

Holy: Invite us... to what?

Oméga: The Apocalypse English Club!

Eddie: We are beginners.

Oméga: Perfect. Beginners are my favorite.

Holy: Why?

Oméga: Because they don't argue—they just learn!

Eddie: Is it difficult?

Oméga: Sometimes.

Holy: Is it fun?

Oméga: Always.

Eddie: Is there homework?

Oméga (pause): ... Let's not talk about that now.

(They laugh a little)

Holy: Okay... we will try.

Act 3: Growth

(≈6-7 min)

(English Club setting — fun atmosphere)

All: Welcome!

Oméga: Rule number one: Speak English!

Club Member: Rule number two: Don't be afraid!

Another Member: Rule number three: If you are afraid... see rule number two!

Eddie (trying): Hello... my name is Eddie... I want to improve my English.

Club Members (clapping): Yesss!

Holy: I am very boring—

(Everyone laughs gently)

Oméga: No no! You are bored, not boring.

Holy: Ah... good. I don't want to be boring.

(At the end of the English club — Faith moment — gentle and respectful)

Oméga: We also share about God here.

Holy: God cares about us?

Oméga: Yes. Even when others laugh at you.

Eddie (softly): Then... I want to know Him.

(Back to school)

Eddie (confident now): Good morning, teacher. I just want to tell you that I've joined an English Club lately and I'm gaining confidence in speaking English.

Teacher Isaia (impressed): Excellent, Eddie! Big improvement!

Sitraka (to Lahatra): What is happening? Did they eat a dictionary?

Lahatra: Maybe English is now inside them...

(Short transition - time passing)
Narrator Fandry

Months passed... then years.

(At the office of the teacher)

Teacher Isaia (excited): Congratulations! You both got scholarships!

Holy: Are you serious??

Eddie: Wait... is this a dream?

Teacher Isaia: If it is, don't wake up!

Holy & Eddie (giving each other a high five): Congratulations!

Act 4: Success & Forgiveness

(≈5-6 min)

Narrator Fandry

Three years later... they come back.

Holy: We studied hard.

Eddie: Now let's help others.

(Office setting)

HR Assistant: Next applicants: Sitraka and Lahatra.

(They enter... freeze when they see them)

Sitraka: Wait... is this... real life?

Lahatra: I think we are in trouble.

Holy (calm): Please sit.

(Awkward silence)

Sitraka: We are... sorry.

Lahatra: Very sorry... like... very very sorry.

Eddie (smiling): We remember everything.

(Pause... tension)

Eddie: But we choose to forgive.

Holy: Everyone can change... like us.

Sitraka (relieved): Thank you...

Lahatra (trying to joke): So... do we get the job?

Holy (smiling): Let's start with the interview first.

Narrator Fandry

From fear... to confidence.
From rejection... to purpose.

All together:
Never give up!
